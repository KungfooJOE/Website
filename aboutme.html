Who?

  • Hello there! As you could probably tell from the website domain name, my name is Joseph Straw and this website serves as my personal creative outlet, as well as a reference guide for some of my projects that I work on.

  • I am currently working on the qualifications needed in becoming a software engineer of some sort. It's been a dream of mine to work in the field of software development, but I have never focused 100% of my effort on achieving it. However, since I finished college, I have a renewed passion and drive to finally achieve my dream. Maybe the yobos at college spooked me into doing something other than manual labour, who knows.

Why the website?

  • To answer the question simply. I wanted to make a start and put my newly acquired skills to use. You might be thinking: “What skills? This website is nothing but a bunch of simple HTML and CSS!” but I did that on purpose. If I really wanted too, I would implement some Javascript to make the site look better, but I believe there is beauty in simplicity. I mean, look at the internet 15 years ago and how much simpler it was to get to where you needed to go. Keyword being "needed". Nowadays, it's so much easier for people, especially zoomers like myself, to get side-tracked and lose sight of what we went onto the internet to look for in the first place!

So what have I accomplished?

  • As of September 2021, I had finished secondary school with a grade 4 in mathematics and grade 5 in english language, as well as a one year GCSE+ course (achieving two grade 6s and a grade 7) at college. So with my next step being a bit of a mystery, I started to work on my skills with programming and web development, which were until then, just a hobby I partook in during my spare time.
